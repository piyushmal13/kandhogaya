Algorithmic Volatility Regime Models

Our proprietary algorithms employ multi-timeframe regime detection filters based on adaptive Standard Deviation curves and Average True Range (ATR) volatilities.

Regime 1: Mean Reversion (Low Volatility) — Enforces narrow grid allocations and trailing locks. Trades are triggered only on statistical outliers beyond the 3.0 Standard Deviation band.

Regime 2: Momentum Breakout (High Volatility) — Trailing stop engines activate, adapting lot sizes dynamically based on standard deviation expansions.

Drawdown Safeguards & Trailing Stops

To maintain strict capital preservation, every compiled Expert Advisor binary (.ex5) contains native risk guardrails:

  • Hard Equity Caps: Automated session shutdown when account balance drops below the customized limit (e.g. 5.0% maximum drawdown threshold).
  • Trailing Stop-Loss: Lot sizes are scaled dynamically, utilizing real-time ATR spreads to reduce exposure during low-liquidity session crossovers.
  • No Custody Risk: Executions occur locally on the user's MT5 terminal setup; zero client funds are held.

ECN Account Integration FAQ

How do I load the EX5 binary file?

Download the compiled file from your client dashboard. Open MT5 > File > Open Data Folder > MQL5 > Experts, paste the file, restart the terminal, and drag it onto your charts.

What account leverage is recommended?

A leverage of 1:100 or higher is optimal to ensure margin limits are not hit during peak grid executions. Conservative accounts can use 1:30 leverage.
